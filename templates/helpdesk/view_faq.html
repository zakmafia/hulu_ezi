FAQ


Q: What are the requirements for a strong password?
Learn more
A: Passwords must be at least eight characters long. Aim for even more strength with fifteen characters or longer. Passwords must include at least 3 of the following: uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters, or certain Unicode characters.
Q: How do I reset my password?
Learn more
A: Go to the password reset page and answer a few security questions. Then, you'll be able to create a new password. As a security precaution, you'll be prompted to create a new password every 6 months.
Q: I'm unable to sign in. Where do I get help?
Learn more
A: Get help with basic tasks like signing in by calling or texting the IT desk from your registered mobile device at any time. If you need help but can't use a registered mobile device, we will ask security questions until we can confirm your identity.
Q: My account was accessed without my knowledge. What do I do?
Learn more
A: Immediately report any suspicious or fraudulent activity with your work accounts or devices by logging an incident or emailing the IT desk. When in doubt, it's best to create an alert than to be impacted by a disruptive security issue. Learn more about data privacy and security practices in the workplace.
